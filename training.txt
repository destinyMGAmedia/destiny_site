Training Structure
The training system consists of seven progressive levels, designed to move members from foundational understanding to leadership and pastoral development.
Level 1 – Foundational Class
Introduces new members to the basic doctrines of the Christian faith and the church.

Level 2 – Destiny Culture (Membership Course)
This class focuses on helping members understand the vision, values, and identity of the ministry.
Topics in this level will include:
Vision
Integrity
Pioneering
Leadership
Excellence
Action
Devotion
Destiny Declaration
Destiny Anthem
Destiny Vision Statement
Destiny Mission Statement
Destiny Founders and Organogram

Level 3 – Ministry Class
This level prepares members for active service in church units. Upon completion, participants may join ministry departments.

Level 4 – Leadership Class
This class prepares members for leadership responsibilities in the church.
Participants may qualify to serve as:
Deacons
Ministry coordinators
Fellowship leaders

Level 5 – Pastoral Class
Also known as the Advanced Leadership Course. It prepares individuals for pastoral and advanced leadership roles.

Level 6 – Advanced Leadership Course 2
Further leadership training designed to deepen ministry and leadership competence.

Level 7 – Advanced Leadership Course 3
The highest leadership development level currently within the system.
